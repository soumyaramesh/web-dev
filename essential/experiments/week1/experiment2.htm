About the Experiment

I have demonstrated a few new HTML5 semantic elements such as section, article, mark, nav, details and summary elements

The <section> element

A section is typically a content group with a heading

A section

Far over the misty mountains cold To dungeons deep, and caverns old We must away ere break of day To seek the pale enchanted gold. The dwarves of yore made mighty spells, While hammers fell like ringing bells In places deep, where dark things sleep, In hollow halls beneath the fells. For ancient king and elvish lord, There many a gleaming golden hoard They shaped and wrought, and light they caught To hide in gems on hilt of sword. On silver necklaces they strung The flowering stars, on crowns they hung The dragon-fire, in twisted wire They meshed the light of moon and sun.

Another section

Far over the misty mountains cold To dungeons deep and caverns old We must away, ere break of day, To claim our long-forgotten gold. Goblets they carved there for themselves And harps of gold; where no man delves There lay they long, and many a song Was sung unheard by men or elves. The pines were roaring on the height, The winds were moaning in the night. The fire was red, it flaming spread; The trees like torches blazed with light. The bells were ringing in the dale And men looked up with faces pale; Then dragon's ire more fierce than fire Laid low their towers and houses frail. The mountain smoked beneath the moon; The dwarves, they heard the tramp of doom. They fled their hall to dying fall Beneath his feet, beneath the moon. Far over the misty mountains grim To dungeons deep and caverns dim We must away, ere break of day, To win our harps and gold from him!

Code Snippet: 

  
            <section>
            Your section content.
            </section>

The <article> element

An article is usually an independent content that makes sense on it's own and is independent of the rest of the site content.

Did you know?

If you have 3 quarters, 4 dimes, and 4 pennies, you have $1.19. You also have the largest amount of money in coins without being able to make change for a dollar.

Code Snippet: 

  
            <article>
            Your article content.
            </article>

The <nav> element

The nav element is used to provide navigational links. I have demonstrated nav in the References section.

Code Snippet:

            <a href="navigation-link1">link1<a>
            <a href="navigation-link2">link2<a>
            </nav>

The <details> and <summary> elements

These elements are used to hide and view details by proving a visible summary for the details.
